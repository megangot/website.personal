Activites

I love staying busy and learning new things. From athletics to music, here are some of the things I'm involved in.

Michigan Sailing Team

Club Sailing at the University of Michigan. As an active member of the sailing team, I sail two or three times a week as a crew. I work with my skipper to create a sucessful boat based in communication, and attended weekly meetings and regattas to connect with the team as a whole.

Tau Epislon Kappa

A co-ed professional fraternity passionate about technology and innovation. TEK is a community of like-minded, hard-working students, all looking to better themselves professionally and academically and to form long-lasting friendships. I am president of the Delta pledge class, making sure that the pledges stay organized and keeping track of involvement.

Choir

Choir has been a constant throughout my life. Currently, I am in the University of Michigan School of Music, Theatre, and Dance Univeristy Choir, and work as a section leader for the Chancel Choir at Bethlem United Church of Christ. In high school, we won a state title in choir both my junior and senior year, and I also won a state title for a competitive duet.
